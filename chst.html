Construction Health and Safety Technician (CHST)

Rating:

★★★★★

Language: English

DESCRIPTION:

The Construction Health and Safety Technician (CHST) certification is a credential awarded to safety professionals specializing in construction. It signifies expertise in managing health and safety hazards on construction sites, including hazard recognition, risk assessment, safety regulations, and safety management systems. CHST-certified professionals play a crucial role in promoting safety awareness and compliance among construction workers and management, contributing to safer and healthier work environments.
